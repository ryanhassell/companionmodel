Both plans include the same core idea: continuity, memory, routines, safety visibility, and a parent portal that explains what is happening. The difference is how much of the experience you want to happen through expressive voice.

How to choose

Choose Chat if...

  • You mainly want a texting companion experience.
  • You want the relationship, memory, and parent portal without prioritizing voice.
  • You want a lower monthly starting point.

How to choose

Choose Voice if...

  • Voice expression would make the relationship much more meaningful.
  • Your child would enjoy a companion that can sound playful, musical, or emotionally expressive.
  • You want the fullest version of the Resona experience.

How billing works

Each plan includes monthly credits. Once those are used, additional usage is billed based on what was actually used.

Included monthly credits

Chat includes $10 in monthly credits. Voice includes $30. Those credits help cover the ongoing companion experience before overage kicks in.

Usage after credits

If your family uses more than the included amount, billing continues based on usage. The billing area shows progress clearly so parents are not left guessing.

Start with the version that fits your family best

You are not choosing between “smart” and “not smart.” You are choosing between a text-first experience and the more expressive Voice experience.
