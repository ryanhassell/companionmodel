Frequently asked questions

Can parents see full conversation history?

Yes. By default, parent accounts can review full timelines for their own child profile.

How does safety monitoring work?

Resona logs detector events, severity, and intervention outcomes in the parent portal.

Can multiple guardians access the same household?

Yes. Authorized co-guardians can be added so oversight responsibilities can be shared securely.

How are charges calculated?

Plans include monthly credits, then meter-based usage applies after credits are consumed.
