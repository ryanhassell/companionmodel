Frequently asked questions

How is Resona different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a great general-purpose AI assistant. Resona is designed around ongoing companionship, memory, routine, and parent visibility, so it feels more like returning to the same familiar presence over time.

Can Resona actually remember things over time?

Yes. Resona is built to carry forward meaningful details, preferences, and relationship context so conversations feel more continuous and less repetitive.

Does it only answer when prompted?

No. Resona is designed to feel more ongoing than a one-off chatbot interaction. It can follow a gentler daily rhythm and fit into routines in a more natural way.

Can parents see the full conversation history?

Yes. Parent accounts can review the timeline for their own child profile, along with safety context and memory highlights that help explain why the interaction unfolded the way it did.

How are charges calculated?

Plans include monthly credits, and additional usage is billed after those credits are used. Each household starts with one active child profile included, and additional active child profiles are billed as a monthly add-on. Parents can track all of it in the billing area instead of guessing.
