Our Story

Founded by Ryan Hassell

Resona was founded to address a gap in consistent social support for individuals with complex developmental needs. My sister has Koolen De-Vries Syndrome, and our family experienced firsthand how difficult it can be to find reliable, meaningful connection in everyday environments.

The platform is designed to provide a warm, stable companion experience while preserving clear parent visibility, strong safety guardrails, and accountable oversight.

How It Works

A clear operating flow from conversation oversight to intervention visibility.

Conversation Oversight

Review what was said, not just a summary score.

  • See inbound/outbound transcript history with timestamps.
  • Inspect memory highlights used to generate replies.
  • Track continuity between SMS and voice-linked sessions.
  • Manage co-guardian visibility for shared supervision.

Safety Controls

Intervention behavior is visible and auditable.

  • View severity and detector reason codes for each event.
  • See action taken when boundaries or risk patterns trigger.
  • Keep clear records of safety interventions over time.
  • Use settings to keep conversation style age-appropriate and stable.
