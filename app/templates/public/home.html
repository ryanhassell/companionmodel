Our Story

Founded by Ryan Hassell

Resona was founded to address a gap in consistent social support for individuals with complex developmental needs. My sister has Koolen De-Vries Syndrome, and our family experienced firsthand how difficult it can be to find reliable, meaningful connection in everyday environments.

The platform is designed to provide a warm, stable companion experience while preserving clear parent visibility, strong safety guardrails, and accountable oversight.

Support the Koolen De-Vries Syndrome Foundation: kdvsfoundation.org/donate/

How It Works

A parent-guided setup that shapes how Resona feels, how it remembers, and how visible everything stays.

Relationship Continuity

Resona keeps building on what it already knows.

  • It remembers how your child prefers to be spoken to.
  • It can hold onto meaningful details instead of starting from zero every time.
  • It can develop a recognizable day-to-day rhythm instead of waiting like a blank inbox.
  • Voice and text still feel like the same relationship.

Parent Visibility

Parents can understand the relationship, not just monitor it.

  • See the full transcript if that is your chosen visibility setting.
  • Review what Resona is remembering and why it matters.
  • See when a moment needed more care and how Resona handled it.
  • Adjust tone, pacing, routines, and boundaries as needs change.
