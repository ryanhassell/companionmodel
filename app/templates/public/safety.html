What parents can monitor

  • Full conversation history for their own child profile.
  • Moments where concern, distress, or boundaries came up.
  • What Resona is remembering and carrying forward over time.

How safety support shows up

  • Resona shifts into a more careful style when a conversation feels more serious.
  • Parents can review the moment later instead of wondering whether anything happened.
  • The goal is supportive companionship with clear adult visibility, not hidden dependence.
